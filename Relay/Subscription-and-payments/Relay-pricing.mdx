# Relay Pricing

## Pricing Update Announcement

> **Important:** Effective October 1, 2024, we will be updating our Relay autoscale pricing to better align with the value and scalability our service offers. The new pricing will apply only to new customers and those renewing on a monthly plan. Customers on a yearly plan will continue with their current pricing until their plan expires. Custom plans will remain unaffected by this update.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When will the new pricing take effect?
The new pricing structure will be effective starting October 1, 2024.

### I'm an existing customer. How does this affect me?
- If you're on a yearly plan, your current pricing will remain unchanged until your plan expires.
- If you're on a monthly plan, the new pricing will apply at your next renewal after October 1, 2024.
- If you're a PRO user, the autoscale price per request over the total requests will increase from $0.003 to $0.030, and the price per request over the minute limit will rise from $0.01 to $0.03.
- If you're a GROWTH user, the autoscale price per request over the total requests will increase from $0.0026 to $0.026.

### What is the difference between monthly and annual billing?
Annual billing offers a 10% discount compared to monthly billing. This can lead to significant savings, especially for the PRO and GROWTH plans.

### What is the autoscale feature?
Autoscale allows your plan to automatically adjust to accommodate increased usage beyond your plan limits, preventing service interruptions.

### How does the gas premium fee work?
The gas premium fee is applied to the cost of gas for transactions on all supported networks. This fee helps cover the costs associated with processing and relaying transactions.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
