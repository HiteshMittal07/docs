title: "Deploy your own rollup"
---

This deployment path is for users who want to deploy their own rollup chain using our 1-click deployment feature. By the end of this guide, you'll have a testnet chain up and running in 30 minutes.

# Steps for Deployment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Getting Started">
    Head over to the official Gelato [RaaS
    dashboard](https://raas.gelato.network/dashboard) & click on "Deploy 1-click
    Rollup"
    <img src="/images/raas-dashboard.png" alt="Gelato RaaS Dashboard" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a Rollup Name">
    Provide a unique name for your rollup. Additionally, you can add a custom
    preferred chain ID for your rollup.
    <img src="/images/configure-chain.png" alt="Gelato RaaS Rollup Name" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Framework/Settlement Layer/Data Availability Layer Selection">
    Choose the rollup stack, settlement layer, and data availability layer for
    your rollup.
    <img src="/images/framework-selection.png" alt="Gelato RaaS Dashboard" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Web3 Services">
    Configure the web3 services for your rollup.
    <img
      src="/images/configure-web3-services.png"
      alt="Gelato RaaS Web3 Services"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Environment Selection">
    Choose the environment you want to deploy your rollup to.
    <img
      src="/images/Environment-selection.png"
      alt="Gelato RaaS Environment Selection"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Deploy">
    Review the chain settings and click next and proceed to pay for your rollup.
  </Step>
</Steps>
